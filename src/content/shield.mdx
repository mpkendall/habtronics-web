<h1 class="text-3xl md:text-4xl text-center font-bold">TinyShield</h1>
<h2 class="text-lg text-center font-bold">Flight Computer Attachment</h2>

<img src="/images/TinyShield_Mounted_Horiz.png" alt="TinyShield Logo" class="w-[95%] md:w-[90%] mx-auto" />

TinyShield turns your Tiny4FSK into feature-rich flight computer, loaded with convenience features. The included JST connectors let you more easily hook up and swap between different external sensors. These connectors are also fully compatible with the Sparkfun Qwiic Connect System.

Save your data onboard using the included MicroSD card slot and use the programmable OLED screen display for previewing your sensor and GPS data live and ensuring full functionality before launch. The shield also has an optional JST XH2.54 LiPo battery connector for powering the whole system. As always, you can alternately apply any power source you like (2.5V-12V) by soldering wires to the VIN and GND pins on the Tiny4FSK. Lastly, the USB-C programming port lets you modify the open-source code and reupload to the board.
